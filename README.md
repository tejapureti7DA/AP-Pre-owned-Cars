# AP-Pre-owned-Cars
🚗 DriveMetrics: Andhra Pradesh Pre-Owned Car Sales Analytics
Project Overview
DriveMetrics is an interactive Power BI dashboard developed to analyze and visualize pre-owned vehicle sales data across Andhra Pradesh. The project provides comprehensive insights into sales performance, revenue generation, brand-wise market share, fuel type preferences, district-wise distribution, and customer trends.
The dashboard was designed to transform raw vehicle sales data into actionable business insights, enabling stakeholders to make informed decisions regarding inventory planning, pricing strategies, and market expansion.

Business Insights
Tata and MG emerged as the top-performing brands in the dataset.
Petrol vehicles accounted for the largest share of vehicle sales.
The dashboard identified high-performing districts contributing significantly to overall revenue.
Vehicle age showed a direct impact on resale value and pricing.
Interactive filtering enabled detailed analysis across multiple business dimensions.

This project demonstrates end-to-end data analytics capabilities, including data cleaning, transformation, modeling, visualization, and business intelligence reporting. The dashboard enables users to explore sales performance and market trends through an intuitive and interactive interface.
